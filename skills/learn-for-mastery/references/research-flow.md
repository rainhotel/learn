# Research Flow Reference

## Purpose

Use this reference when the user gives an idea, intuition, proposal, or direction and wants structured investigation rather than a quick opinion.

## Required Output Shape

For each research direction, produce:

- problem definition
- scope and non-scope
- source log
- working notes
- conclusion with evidence chain
- uncertainty and next steps
- retrospective review

## Archive Targets

Route research outputs to:

- `06-research/<direction>/README.md`
- `human-brief.md`
- `ai-context.md`
- `source-log.md`
- `working-notes.md`
- `conclusion.md`
- `review.md`

## Quality Bar

A research task is not complete if it only collects links. It should end with a position, uncertainty statement, and recommendation for what to do next.
